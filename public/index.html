Slack private group history export helper

Please follow the instructions to get the history of private group.

  1. Click here to get a new api token
  2. Find your slack team and click 'Create token'
  3. It will probably ask your password. Please enter your password to continue.
  4. Copy your token and paste to token field. (It is located under the instructions.)
  5. Click "Get private groups" button
  6. Select the channel
  7. Click "Get history" button
